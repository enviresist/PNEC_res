# Original data

Original data on minimum inhibiting concentrations of antibiotics and related information (e.g. Ecoffs) were obtained from the official EUCAST website (https://mic.eucast.org/search/). Please contact EUCAST (https://www.eucast.org/) for questions about the (re)-use of this data.

Data on the cost of antibiotic resistances were extracted from scientific publications. The respective original source is always indicated using the digital object identifier (DOI).
